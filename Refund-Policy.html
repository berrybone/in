Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing Berrybone for your healthcare needs. We strive to provide exceptional service and support to our valued users. Our refund policy is designed to ensure transparency, fairness, and satisfaction for all customers.

1. Refund Eligibility

We understand that circumstances may arise where a refund is necessary. Refunds may be eligible under the following conditions:

  • Subscription Cancellation: Users who cancel their subscription within the designated refund period are eligible for a full or prorated refund, depending on the terms of their subscription plan.
  • Service Disruption: In the event of a service disruption or unavailability that significantly impacts user experience, eligible users may request a refund for the affected period.

    • 2. Refund Process

    To request a refund, users must submit a refund request through our designated support channel, clearly stating the reason for the refund and providing any relevant details or documentation.


    Upon receiving the refund request, our support team will review the request and assess its eligibility based on the terms outlined in this policy.


    If the refund request is approved, the refund will be processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction, unless otherwise specified.


    3. Non-Refundable Items

    Certain items or services may not be eligible for refunds, including but not limited to:


  • Digital Downloads: Products or services that have been digitally downloaded or accessed by the user.
  • Consumable Goods: Items that are consumed or used during the course of service, such as virtual goods or in-app purchases.
  • Customized Services: Services that have been customized or tailored to the user's specifications, including personalized health plans or consultations.

    • 4. Dispute Resolution

    If a user disagrees with the outcome of their refund request, they may escalate the issue to our management team for further review and resolution. Our team will work diligently to address any concerns and reach a fair and satisfactory resolution.


    5. Contact Us

    For any questions, concerns, or refund requests, please contact our support team at Contact Page. Our dedicated team is available to assist you and ensure your experience with Berrybone is positive and fulfilling.


    6. Policy Updates

    Berrybone reserves the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes to the policy will be reflected on this page, and users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for updates.


    We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in adhering to our refund policy. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are committed to providing you with the highest level of service and support.


    Thank you for choosing Berrybone.
